## Color Scheme

**Primary Colors (ToqueWorks Brand):**
- Primary Blue: `#2563EB` (blue-600)
- Success Green: `#059669` (green-600)
- Warning Red: `#DC2626` (red-600)

**Neutral Colors:**
- Gray 50: `#F9FAFB` (background)
- Gray 200: `#E5E7EB` (borders)
- Gray 600: `#4B5563` (secondary text)
- Gray 900: `#111827` (primary text)

**Status Colors:**
- Selected: Blue 50 background + Blue 400 border
- Under Budget: Green 50 background + Green 400 border
- Over Budget: Red 50 background + Red 400 border

## Typography

**Headings:**
- H1: 3xl (30px), bold
- H2: 2xl (24px), bold
- H3: xl (20px), semibold

**Body:**
- Regular text: base (16px)
- Small text: sm (14px)
- Tiny text: xs (12px)

## Interactive Elements

**Buttons:**
- Primary: Blue background, white text, hover darker
- Secondary: Gray background, gray text, hover darker
- Success: Green background, white text, hover darker

**Recipe Cards:**
- Default: White bg, gray border, hover shadow
- Selected: Blue bg, blue border, shadow
- Hover: Border changes to blue

**Input Fields:**
- Border: Gray 300
- Focus: Blue ring + Blue border
- Padding: 4px vertical, 4px horizontal
